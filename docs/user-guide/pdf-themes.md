# PDF Theme System

Clara now supports **8 beautiful color themes** for your exported PDFs! Each theme has been carefully designed with unique color palettes and styling to make your puzzles stand out.

## Available Themes

### 1. **Classic** (Default)
- **Colors**: Traditional black and white
- **Best for**: Professional, timeless puzzles
- **Description**: Clean and simple, perfect for all ages

### 2. **Vibrant**
- **Colors**: Purple, pink, and gold accents
- **Best for**: Eye-catching, energetic puzzle books
- **Description**: Colorful and fun with bold borders

### 3. **Ocean**
- **Colors**: Cool blues and aqua tones
- **Best for**: Calming, water-themed puzzles
- **Description**: Refreshing blue palette with light backgrounds

### 4. **Forest**
- **Colors**: Natural greens and earth tones
- **Best for**: Nature-themed puzzle books
- **Description**: Peaceful green colors inspired by nature

### 5. **Sunset**
- **Colors**: Warm oranges and pinks
- **Best for**: Warm, inviting puzzle collections
- **Description**: Sunset-inspired warm color palette

### 6. **Royal**
- **Colors**: Elegant purples and gold
- **Best for**: Premium, sophisticated puzzle books
- **Description**: Regal purple with gold accents

### 7. **Kids Fun**
- **Colors**: Bright pink, orange, and green
- **Best for**: Children's puzzle books
- **Description**: Playful and bright with thick borders

### 8. **Minimal**
- **Colors**: Clean gray tones
- **Best for**: Modern, minimalist designs
- **Description**: Subtle grays with thin, elegant lines

## How to Use

1. **Select a Theme**: In the sidebar, find the "PDF Theme" dropdown
2. **Choose Your Style**: Pick from 8 different themes
3. **Generate Puzzle**: Create your word search
4. **Export**: Click "Export PDF" - your theme will be applied!

## Theme Features

Each theme includes:
- ✅ **Custom Colors**: Grid borders, letters, backgrounds, and highlights
- ✅ **Unique Styling**: Border thickness, font sizes, and spacing
- ✅ **Answer Key Colors**: Matching highlight and line colors for solutions
- ✅ **Professional Quality**: All themes are KDP print-ready

## Theme Customization

Each theme controls:
- **Grid Border Color & Width**
- **Letter Color**
- **Background Color**
- **Title Color**
- **Word List Color**
- **Answer Key Highlight Color**
- **Answer Key Line Color**
- **Font Sizes** (title, grid, word list)

## Tips for Choosing Themes

- **Kids' Books**: Use "Kids Fun" or "Vibrant"
- **Adult Puzzles**: Try "Classic", "Minimal", or "Royal"
- **Themed Books**: Match the theme to your content:
  - Ocean puzzles → "Ocean" theme
  - Nature puzzles → "Forest" theme
  - Romantic puzzles → "Sunset" theme

## Example Workflow

```
1. Enter your words
2. Set difficulty to "Medium"
3. Choose shape: "Circle"
4. Select PDF Theme: "Ocean"
5. Generate Puzzle
6. Export PDF
7. Get a beautiful blue-themed puzzle ready for KDP!
```
